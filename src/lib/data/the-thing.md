Ben Grimm, the Thing, is a rock-solid Vanguard with immense strength and durability. He excels at absorbing damage, disrupting enemy formations with powerful ground-based attacks, and protecting his teammates.

[//]: # (![image]&#40;{{.image}}&#41;)

## Abilities

### The Thing

**Primary Fire: Rock Solid Punch**
*   **Description:** Delivers a powerful punch, dealing high melee damage and a small area-of-effect knockback.
*   **Casting:** Instant
*   **Ammo:** Infinite
*   **Fire Rate:** 1.1s
*   **Critical Hit:** Yes
*   **Range:** Melee

**Ability 1: Ground Pound**
*   **Description:** Slams his fists into the ground, creating a shockwave that damages and briefly stuns enemies in a cone in front of him.
*   **Cooldown:** 9s

**Ability 2: Boulder Toss**
*   **Description:** Rips a chunk of the ground and hurls it, dealing heavy damage to the first enemy hit and knocking them back.
*   **Cooldown:** 12s

**Ultimate: It's Clobberin' Time!**
*   **Description:** The Thing enters a berserker state, gaining increased health, damage, and a continuous area-of-effect tremor that slows and damages nearby enemies.
*   **Ultimate Charge:** High

## Strategy

*   **Frontline Anchor:** The Thing is designed to be at the forefront of battle. Use your high health and damage to draw enemy fire and protect your squishier allies.
*   **Crowd Control:** Ground Pound is excellent for interrupting enemy abilities, stunning multiple targets, or creating space for your team to advance.
*   **Disruption:** Boulder Toss can be used to knock back dangerous enemies, separate them from their team, or interrupt their positioning.
*   **Ultimate Push:** "It's Clobberin' Time!" is perfect for initiating a team fight or pushing through a heavily defended area. The continuous tremor makes it difficult for enemies to escape.

## Team-Up

*   **Fantastic Four Formation (with Mister Fantastic):** The Thing and Mister Fantastic combine to create a powerful defensive formation, with Thing absorbing damage while Reed stretches to protect allies.
*   **Fastball Special (with Wolverine):** The Thing hurls an allied Wolverine at an enemy, dealing massive damage and stunning the target.

## Achievements

*   **Rock Solid:** Absorb 60,000 damage in a single match.
*   **Clobberin' Time!:** Get 3 eliminations while "It's Clobberin' Time!" is active.
*   **Earthquake:** Stun 3 or more enemies with a single Ground Pound 5 times.
*   **Heavy Hitter:** Deal 25,000 damage with Rock Solid Punch.
